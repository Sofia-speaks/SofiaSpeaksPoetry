Poem Excerpts

    
      

Excerpt from "A Trust Misplaced"

      

In shadows where whispers weave,
         Promises made, yet hearts deceive,
         A trust misplaced, a bond betrayed,
         Lessons linger in the price we paid.

    
    
      

Excerpt from "Strong Women"

      

She stands when storms rage wild and loud,
         Her soul unbroken, fierce and proud,
         A legacy of love and grace,
         Strength eternal in her embrace.

    
    
      

Excerpt from "I'd Rather"

      

I’d rather stumble chasing dreams,
         Than sit in silence, split at seams,
         I’d rather fight than fade away,
         And greet the dawn of each new day.
